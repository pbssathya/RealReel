# Our Principles

## 1. Creator First

Every decision should make life simpler for creators.

Technology exists to serve creativity, not the other way around.

---

## 2. Workflow Before Providers

RealReel owns the workflow.

AI providers contribute intelligence.

Providers may change.

The workflow remains.

---

## 3. Provider Independence

No AI provider should become a permanent dependency.

Creators should be free to choose the technologies that best suit their needs.

---

## 4. Cloud First

RealReel is designed primarily for cloud deployment.

Local execution exists for development, testing, learning, and experimentation.

---

## 5. Business Before Technology

Business concepts should drive the architecture.

Technology should support the domain, never define it.

---

## 6. Simplicity Wins

Prefer simple, understandable solutions over clever complexity.

Every module should have a clear responsibility.

---

## 7. Build for Extension

Adding new providers, workflows, or capabilities should require minimal changes to existing code.

---

## 8. Documentation is Part of the Product

Documentation evolves together with the software.

Clear documentation is a feature, not an afterthought.

---

## 9. Test Before Trust

Features should be validated through working examples before becoming part of the production workflow.

Confidence comes from verification.

---

## 10. Build for the Long Term

Choose solutions that remain valuable years from now.

Avoid short-term optimisations that create long-term complexity.

---

# Our Guiding Question

Before introducing a feature or architectural change, ask:

> **Does this help creators transform meaningful ideas into publish-ready content more simply, more openly, and more effectively?**

If the answer is "no", reconsider the design.
